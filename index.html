[ ✦ XP // how it works ]

Your XP score

XP is your prize-draw weight — the more XP you hold, the bigger your share of every draw. It's earned automatically just by holding any StakeNova product. No claiming, no staking, no action required.

🎟️
Ticket weight, not a lottery ticket
Your XP doesn't give you one entry — it's your proportion of the pool. If you hold 1% of total XP at draw time, you win roughly 1% of draws over time. More XP = better expected value, always.
⏱️
Time-weighted loyalty
XP accrues every day. Holding 10 SOL for 10 days earns twice as much as holding 10 SOL for 5 days. Long-term stakers are naturally rewarded over late whales.
🔄
50% epoch decay — keeps it fair
After each Solana epoch (~2.5 days), half your XP decays. This prevents early depositors from permanently dominating draws and keeps every new depositor competitive from day one.
🏆
XP leaderboard = bonus multipliers
Top 10 on the leaderboard get a 1.5×–3× XP multiplier applied to future draws. Rank #1 enters every draw with 3× weight — without needing more capital, just consistency.
